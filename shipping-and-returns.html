1. Your ClickBank Order ID

2. Your Full Name, and Full Address where the shipment was received

3. Your Email Address and Phone Number

4. Your original packing slip (if available)


In order for your refund** to be processed the product must arrive at our fulfillment facility within sixty (60) days of the original purchase date. Failure to include all the bottles purchased be they empty, full or partially full (including any "bonus", "free" or "gifted" bottles), or failure to clearly write your order details as specified above so that a member of our fulfillment center staff can clearly read and allocate your return may both result in our inability to refund you. The buyer will pay for return shipping at all times.


Once our fulfillment center has received the package and relayed the correct information to us, you will be issued a refund. Your refund will be credited back to your bank account/card and may take up to 3-5 business days to show in your statement, depending on the speed of the processing bank.
